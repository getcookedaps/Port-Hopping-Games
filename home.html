Tips for hiding games during class

  • Specific to this site:
    Press the grave accent (`) to cover the game with a screenshot of Google Classroom for when teachers are watching your screen, then press "esc" to return to games
  • Common Methods:
    Keyboard shortcuts: On Chromebooks, press the "Search" key and the "=" key at the same time. This will create an additional desk to hide sensitive tabs. Press "search" + "[" or "]" to navigate between them. Press "search" + "shift" + "[" or "]" to move tabs between different desks. There is a maximum of eight desks. "ctrl" + "1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, or 9" will switch to the corresponding tab for quickly hiding tabs.
